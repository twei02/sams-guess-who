guess who

Instructions

Goal of the game: Guess your opponent’s card before they guess your card.

To start, choose a name from the dropdown list to be your mystery card. Use the chat box, or speak out loud, and take turns asking one “yes” or “no” question to one another. Based on your opponent’s answer, use the grid of images to eliminate characters that cannot be their chosen card. To eliminate a card, click on the image, and it will turn black and white. If you made a mistake, click again and it will return to full color.

Example: you might ask “Does the person on your card have black hair?”. If your opponent responds “yes”, you can click on all the people with non-black hair, leaving only people with black hair on your grid as possible answers. If “no”, then you can eliminate the people with black hair on your grid.

Think you figured out your opponent’s card? You can make a guess instead of asking a question. Tell your opponent you are making your guess, and if you are incorrect, you lose the game. If you are correct, you win!
