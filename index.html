About The Game

Welcome to Guess Who: The Sam Edition. This is a spinoff of the classic Guess Who board game with customized characters for Sam's birthday (Happy Birthday Sam!) Featuring new gameboards and an upgraded chat feature, this builds off of the original Guess Who project, created by myself and two peers, Nathan and Halle, during Google's Computer Science Summer Institute.
